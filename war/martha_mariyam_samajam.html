On behalf of MMVS it is my privilege to present the MMVS annual report for the year 2016. I would like to thank Almighty God for all the blessings showered on our parish. I take this opportunity to thank our Achen Rev. Fr. Babu K. Mathew for all the support, guidance and teachings for all Samajam activities.MMVS gives women in the church a platform where they get an opportunity to study about our church traditions, history and the Bible. During 2016, MMVS got the chance to learn the book of Revelation. Thanks to our Achan who took classes about the book of Revelation almost every Sunday after the Holy Qurbana.

Apart from these classes we had five official meeting of the Samajam during the year presided over by Rev. Fr. Babu K. Mathew. We gave donations to the following charities:

1. Charity led by Dr. Gabriel Mar Gregorios, Trivandrum. $ 500

2. Charity for cancer patients in India $ 150

3. Charity to poor and needy in India $ 250

4. Charity through NE American Diocese $ 150

Total charity amount given during 2016 is $1,400.

The MMVS Annual Conference was held on Oct. 15th, 2016, at St. Thomas Orthodox Church, Unrue, Philadelphia. The main theme of the conference was based the book of Revelation. Thirteen members from our church attended the conference and the Quiz competition. We got 4th place in the quiz competition. There was raffle drawing and graduation ceremony for Divya Bhodhanam. From our church, four members completed Level 3 and one member completed Level 1. MMVS supported all spiritual organizations in our church. We also supported the Food bank in Elizabeth, NJ. Many members and families helped pack the food for the needy people. Other activities included donating school bags for the needy children and conducting prayer for the sick people.

On behalf of MMVS, I express my sincere respect and gratitude to our Metropolitan H.G. Zachariah Mar Nicholovas, Our Vicar Rev. Fr. Babu K. Mathew, Treasurer, Secretary, all spiritual organizations and all members of our church for their continued support and prayers.

Sincerely submitted by,

Jaya Das

Secretary.
