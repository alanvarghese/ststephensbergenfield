Martha Maria Samajam

I would like to thank Our Vicar Rev. Fr. Babu K. Mathew and all the members for giving me the opportunity to be the Secretary of Martha Maria Samajam of St. Stephen's Malankara Orthodox Church, Midland Park, NJ for the year 2014.

In the year 2014, MMVS held one meeting every month presided by our Vicar Rev. Fr. Babu K. Mathew. Each meeting included devotional songs, Bible reading, message that led us to spiritual growth and concluded with prayers.

Our Samajam contributed total amount of $850 for the Northeast American Dioceses MMVS towards its charity contribution fund. Four members went to the Regional Food Donation Center in Elizabeth, NJ where they helped package food for the needy people. Also, Mrs. Lovely Philip donated two school bag and supplies for the Donation Center. We also helped one kidney patient in India by sending $250 for his medical expenses. We also visited sick people at their home and hospital to pray for their recovery. Martha Maria Samajam donated $5,000 for steps construction project of our church.

On October 25th MMVS Annual Conference was held at St. John's Orthodox Church, Orangeburg, NY. Fourteen members attended the conference and participated in the Quiz competition, raffle drawing and graduation ceremony. Raffle ticket was won by our own member, Mrs. Lovely Philip.

Total of twelve members attended the Divya Bodhanam classes and three of them completed Level 1 and six completed Level 2. This year's Divya Bodhanam classes will be held at our church St. Stephen's Malankara Orthodox Church, Midland Park on the following days: Feb 14, Mar 21 and May 2.

On behalf of MMVS, I extend our sincere respect, love and gratitude, to our Dioceses Metropolitan His Grace Zachariah Mar Nicholovos and our Vicar for their valuable guidance, support and prayers.

Sincerely,
Mrs. Rachel Mathew
Secretary - Martha Maria Samajam
