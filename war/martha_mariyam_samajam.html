Activities for 2015:

In the year 2015, MMVS held meetings presided by our Vicar Rev. Fr. Babu K. Mathew. Each meeting included devotional songs, Bible reading, message that led us to spiritual growth and concluded with prayers.

This year the MMVS members participated in various activities conducted by our church, NJ-Staten Island area of the North East American diocese.

St. Stephen's Midland Park MMVS contributed a total amount of $1050 to the Northeast American Diocese MMVS which includes donation towards charity and raffle. The MMVS Annual Conference for 2015 was held on Saturday, October 17th, at St. Thomas Orthodox church, Levittown, NY. Sixteen members from our church attended the conference and participated in the Quiz competition, raffle drawing and Divya Bodhanam graduation ceremony. Five members graduated Level 3 Divya Bodhanam classes. Level 1 and Level 2 participants will continue to attend the next level in 2016. This year's Raffle ticket winner was once again, Mrs. Lovely Philip from our church.

As part of the MMVS Chapter of the NJ-Staten Island area about 22 members from our church which included youth members from MGOCSM and the MMVS members volunteered at the Regional Food Donation Center in Hillside, NJ. They helped package food for the needy people. In yet another event organized by the NJ-Staten Island MMVS chapter, Mrs. Rachel Mathew assisted the MMVS area secretary in shopping for school supplies in support of the back to school program organized by the Salvation Army. Mrs. Lovely Philip donated two school bags containing all school supplies for this purpose.

On behalf of the MMVS St. Stephen's Midland Park church, I extend our sincere respect, love and gratitude, to our Diocesan Metropolitan His Grace Zachariah Mar Nicholovos and our Vicar Fr. Babu K. Mathew for their valuable guidance, support, leadership and prayers.
