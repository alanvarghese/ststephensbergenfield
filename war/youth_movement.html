Youth Movement / MGOCSM report

"A Church without Youth is a Church without a future. Moreover, Youth without a Church is Youth without a future." - Pope Shenouda III, Pope & Patriarch of Alexandria.

Vice President - Anol Abraham
Secretary - Sara Cherian
Joint Secretary - Elijah Mathew
Treasurer - Timothy Mathew

In the year of 2014 we have tried to be as active as possible by involving ourselves in many activities as possible. Many of our members actively participated in helping organize the OVBS in our church this year and many of us taught the classes. Also, led by Anol Abraham we were able to run the Operation Christmas Child for a second year in a row. We managed to put together over 100 shoe boxes filled with supplies to send to unfortunate children in third-world countries who do not have access to these necessities. A few of the boys of the MGOCSM played in basketball tournaments against fellow churches. They also went to a coffee house in Long Island to perform Christian songs in front of members from fellow churches. Through bake sales and our t-shirt sales, we were able to raise a good amount of money, which we plan to use in the near future to help various charities. For this coming summer, we have also planned to do a car wash and volunteer at a food bank as well as a soup kitchen. On the spiritual side of things, we attended retreats at other fellow churches, had our very own youth retreat, and held our 5th Annual Fast over, which were all blessed and wonderful experiences. With God's grace, we hope to accomplish more tasks and participate in more events, such as community service and fundraising for our church. We kindly ask for everyone's prayers so that we can have another fruitful year.

Sincerely,
Sara Cherian (Secretary)
