Sunday School

"The Fear of the Lord is The Beginning of Wisdom."     Proverbs - 1:7

  Sunday school Timing: 9.00 am to 10.15 am

With the grace of God Almighty, St. Stephen's Orthodox Church has a full fledged Sunday school consisting of 58 students and 26 teachers attending classes from Kindergarten to 12th grade. Following are the teachers: Abey. K.Tharian, Leena Varghese, Aju Tharian, Aleyamma John, Jimmy John, Leena George, Valsamma Joseph, Jinu Kurian, Elizabeth Mathew, Joshila Mathew, Lincy Thomas, Saly Abraham, Latha Panicker, Jasmine Biju, Saramma Jacob, Sunita Varghese, Nizy Roy, Aleyamma Varghese, Rinzy Roy, Bindu John, Smitha Mathai, Timothy Mathew, Alan Varghese, Rinsu George, Shelby Varghese and Titus Mathew.

Two students from grade 5, five students from grade 8, and three students from grade 10 registered and passed the Centralized Examination conducted by the North East American Diocese. Alina Tharian scored 6th rank and Jeremy Kurian scored 8th rank at the Diocesan level Centralized Grade 5 exam. In Grade 8, Jonathan Kurian ranked 9th in the centralized exam. In the TTC examination, Mrs. Aleyamma Varghese also scored the 9th rank. Congratulations to all the top scorers.

To encourage our Sunday school children for better academic performances, we have introduced cash awards from our church sponsors for the highest scoring children in fifth, eighth and tenth grade centralized examinations. These children should score 80 or more to be eligible for this award. This year, Mrs. Ponnama Mammen Memorial $100 Cash award was given to Alina Tharian from grade 5 by Johnson& Shirley Thomas and P. K. Koshy Memorial $50 Cash award was given to Jeremy Kurian from Grade 5 by Sunny & Leena Varghese. Mr.O.M.Kunjukunju memorial $100 cash award sponsored by Titus Mathew & family was given to Jonathan Kurian from 8th grade and Mr. Sam David memorial $50 Cash award was given to Stephen John from 8th grade by Mrs. Jomy David. Mr & Mrs. T. K. Mathew memorial $100 cash award sponsored by Abey Tharian & family was given to Ashin Alex from 10th grade.

Our Sunday school children displayed commendable performance in the area talent competitions held at St. Baselios Gregorios Orthodox Church, North Plainfield, NJ. We captured nine first prizes and three second prizes in the area talent competitions. Congratulations to Alison Tharian, Andrew Philip, Julia Mathew, Elijah Mathew and Stephanie John for winning prizes during the North East American Diocese final talent competitions held at Saunders High school, New York. Alison Tharian won first prize in both solo song and story-telling competitions and also awarded the Susan Thampi memorial trophy during this event.

OVBS-2015 was held on July 23 to 25 in our church with the help and cooperation of Sunday school teachers, Marthamariam Samajam Members, Church Office bearers and many well wishers. Vicar Rev. Fr. Babu K. Mathew delivered the keynote message based on the theme "Seek the things which are above" (Colossians 3:1).

Christmas Eve Program was held with great joy and celebration with the help and participation of all Sunday school children, teachers and parents on Dec 24th 2015. Children performed dances, dramas, instrumental music and sang many carol songs during the event. A charity drive called "hats for charity" raised $300 by Sunday school children during Xmas time. This money will be donated for educational expenses of a needy student selected by ICON charities.

Sincere thanks to all members of our church for their continued prayers, support and encouragement of our Sunday school. Also on behalf of the Sunday school teachers and students, I express my deepest gratitude towards our Vicar Rev. Fr. Babu K. Mathew for his consistent care, guidance and prayers that make our Sunday school, the foundation of Orthodox faith.

Mr.Abey K.Tharian Sunday School Principal
